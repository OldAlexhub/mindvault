# Feature Graphic Design Notes

**Required dimensions:** 1024 x 500 pixels
**Format:** PNG or JPEG
**Purpose:** Google Play Store feature graphic (appears at top of store listing)

---

## Design Concept

### Primary Visual

- Background: Deep dark environment suggesting a vault interior or digital underground space
- Central element: A large vault door rendered with a metallic, industrial look, viewed straight-on or at a slight angle
- Lock element: A glowing digital combination lock or keypad on the vault door face, with a faint electric or neon glow in gold or cyan tones
- Depth effect: Subtle radial glow or vignette from the center outward, giving a dramatic spotlight feel

### Typography

- App name "MindVault" displayed prominently across the center or lower center of the graphic
- Font style: Bold, geometric, modern sans-serif
- Color: Gold, white, or bright neon (do NOT use standard plain white if it blends into background)
- Optional tagline below the name: "Unlock Every Vault" or "Challenge Your Mind"
- Tagline font: Lighter weight, smaller size, same color family

### Color Palette

- Background: Very dark navy, charcoal, or near-black
- Vault door: Dark metallic gray or dark steel with subtle highlights
- Accent color 1: Gold (#FFD700 or similar warm gold)
- Accent color 2: Neon teal or electric blue for the glowing lock element
- Text: White or bright gold for maximum contrast

### Glow and Effects

- Subtle glow around the lock dial
- Light lens flare or particle sparks near the lock mechanism
- Slight gradient from very dark edges to slightly lighter center
- Optional digital or circuit-board micro-texture on the vault door surface

---

## What NOT to Include

- Do NOT include any IQ score, IQ number, or text claiming to measure intelligence
- Do NOT include children or cartoon characters
- Do NOT include medical, brain scan, or clinical imagery
- Do NOT include real human faces
- Do NOT include text saying "Raise your IQ" or any cognitive ability claim
- Do NOT include the price, "Free", or any store-related call-to-action text inside the graphic (Google prohibits this)
- Do NOT use low-contrast text that blends into the background

---

## Tools and Suggestions

- Canva (free tier), Adobe Photoshop, Figma, or GIMP can produce this
- Search for "vault door" or "safe door" stock images on Unsplash, Pexels, or Pixabay (verify license)
- Use a dark overlay on any stock photo to integrate it into the dark color scheme
- Export at exactly 1024 x 500 pixels

---

## File Naming

Save the final file as: feature-graphic-1024x500.png (or .jpg)
Upload to Google Play Console under: Store presence > Main store listing > Featured graphic
