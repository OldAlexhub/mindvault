## Full Description

MindVault is a fast-paced brain puzzle game that challenges your logic, memory, word skills, and pattern recognition across four distinct Vault Modes. Tackle the Daily Vault for a fresh challenge every day, track your Vault Score locally, and unlock new themes as you improve. No login, no account, and core gameplay works offline. Share your best results with friends using the Android share sheet whenever you feel like showing off. Contains banner ads. MindVault is entertainment software and is NOT a certified IQ test or professional cognitive assessment. Think you can crack every vault? The lock is waiting.
