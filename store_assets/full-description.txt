MindVault is a brain puzzle game built for speed and variety. Take on four Vault Modes: Logic, Memory, Word, and Pattern. Return daily for the Daily Vault, a fresh set of puzzles that resets every 24 hours. All your scores and streaks are saved locally on your device. No account, no login, and core gameplay works offline. Unlock new themes as you level up. Share your best Vault Score with friends via the Android share sheet anytime you like. Contains banner ads. MindVault is entertainment software, not a certified IQ test. Think you can crack every vault?
