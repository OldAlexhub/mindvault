Version 1.0.0 - First Release

MindVault is now available on Google Play.

This first release includes:

- Four Vault Modes: Logic, Memory, Word, and Pattern puzzles
- Daily Vault with a fresh challenge every 24 hours
- Local scoring and streak tracking (stored on your device, no login required)
- Multiple unlockable themes
- Result sharing via the Android share sheet
- Offline core gameplay
- Banner ads via Google AdMob

Thank you for playing MindVault. Feedback and reviews are welcome.
