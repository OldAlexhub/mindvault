# Privacy Policy

**App Name:** MindVault
**Developer:** Old Alex Hub
**Effective Date:** 2025-01-01
**Contact:** info@oldalexhub.com

---

## Overview

MindVault is an IQ-style brain puzzle game for Android. It is entertainment software designed to challenge your logic, memory, word, and pattern skills. MindVault is NOT a real IQ test, a certified intelligence assessment, or a professional cognitive evaluation tool. Results are for entertainment purposes only.

---

## No Account Required

MindVault does not require you to create an account or log in. There is no registration, no user profile synced to any server, and no cloud backup of any kind. You can use the app entirely without identifying yourself.

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

Old Alex Hub, the developer of MindVault, does not collect, receive, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII). The developer has no backend server, no database, and no analytics pipeline connected to this app.

The following data types are NOT collected by the developer:

- Name, email address, or contact information
- Location data (GPS or network-based)
- Camera or microphone data
- Device contacts or calendar entries
- Browsing history or search history
- Financial or payment information
- Health or fitness data
- Login credentials of any kind

---

## Data Stored Locally on Your Device

MindVault stores the following data locally on your device using Android AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to the developer or any third-party server controlled by the developer.

- Game scores and Vault Scores
- Puzzle streak counters
- App settings and preferences
- Theme unlock status

All local data is stored on your device only. You own it and you can delete it at any time.

---

## INTERNET Permission

MindVault requests the INTERNET permission for two purposes:

**1. AdMob Banner Ads**
MindVault displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK). These ads require an internet connection. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may process advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) and diagnostic data in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. This data collection is governed by Google, not by Old Alex Hub.

**2. Public Puzzle Data Refresh**
Some puzzle content may be refreshed by making read-only calls to public, unauthenticated APIs including the Datamuse API, REST Countries API, Open Trivia Database, Free Dictionary API, and World Bank Indicators API. These calls do not include any personal information, device identifiers, or user data. They are purely read operations that fetch publicly available reference data and cache it locally for gameplay.

## Data Deletion

Because all app data is stored locally on your device and the developer does not receive any personal data, there is nothing to request deletion of from the developer's side.

To delete your local game data:

- **In-app reset:** Open MindVault, go to Settings, and use the Reset / Clear Data option to erase all local scores, streaks, and settings.
- **Android system:** Go to Android Settings > Apps > MindVault > Storage > Clear Data. This will permanently delete all local app data.
